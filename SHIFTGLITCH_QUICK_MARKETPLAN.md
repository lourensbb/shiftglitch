## STEP 1 — PRODUCT HUNT: Schedule Your Launch

**URL:** producthunt.com
**Time to set up:** 45 minutes
**Expected result:** 500–2,000 targeted visitors on launch day

Product Hunt is the single most powerful free launch channel for a product like ShiftGlitch. Half a million early adopters, developers, students, and educators get emailed every day with new launches. The cyberpunk aesthetic will stand out hard against the usual wave of SaaS dashboards and AI wrappers.

**Exactly what to do:**
1. Create a maker account at producthunt.com
2. Add ShiftGlitch: tagline = *"The cyberpunk study OS built on actual learning science"*
3. Upload a clean banner (1270×760px) showing the BIOS boot animation
4. Add a 10-second GIF of a Pomodoro session and a Leitner deck
5. Schedule for a Tuesday or Wednesday at 12:01am PT (peak traffic day)
6. On launch day, reply to every comment within the first two hours — this drives the algorithm

One good Product Hunt launch puts ShiftGlitch in front of more targeted users than six months of SEO.

## STEP 4 — MICROSOFT BING ADS: $500 Free Credit, Almost Nobody Uses This

**URL:** ads.microsoft.com
**Time to set up:** 30 minutes
**Expected result:** Weeks of paid traffic at zero cost

New Microsoft Advertising accounts regularly receive up to $500 in free ad credit. This is not a trick — it is a standard new-advertiser incentive that most indie developers never claim.

**Why Bing specifically for ShiftGlitch:**
School computers frequently block Google but allow Bing. Students on school devices who search for study tools land on Bing results. This is the channel your competitors are not touching.

**Keywords to target:**
- "best study app for students"
- "spaced repetition app"
- "revision app GCSE"
- "active recall study tool"
- "Anki alternative"

Set max CPC to £0.10–£0.20. Your $500 credit will run for weeks. Set a daily cap of $10 so nothing surprises you.

## STEP 6 — QUORA ANSWERS: Rank on Google for Years Without Writing a Blog

**URL:** quora.com
**Time to set up:** 20 minutes per answer
**Expected result:** Each answer can accumulate 5,000–50,000 views over 12–24 months

Quora answers rank on page 1 of Google for highly specific questions — and they stay there. Find these questions and write genuinely helpful, detailed answers. Then mention ShiftGlitch naturally at the end.

**Target questions:**
- "What is the best spaced repetition app for students?"
- "What study methods actually work for GCSE?"
- "How do I stop forgetting what I revise?"
- "What is the blurting method?"
- "Is Anki good for GCSE students?"
- "What is active recall and how do I use it?"

Write three answers this week. Come back monthly and write three more. The investment is small. The compounding is real.
