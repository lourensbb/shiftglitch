# Synapse – MCQ Mission System (FINAL Design Direction)

Thank you for the work done so far. The structure, UI quality, and learning workflows are strong. I now want to clearly define **how the 100-question MCQ bank should function inside Synapse**, so it enhances learning rather than turning into a trivia or XP-driven system.

This is a refinement of direction, not a reset.

---

## 1. Purpose of the MCQ Bank

The MCQ content is **not** intended to be a standalone quiz game.

Instead, it functions as:

* a **navigation checkpoint**
* a **diagnostic tool**
* a **gate** that reveals learning gaps

MCQs should **never be the primary activity**. They validate learning *after* work has been done.

---

## 2. Structural Overview

The MCQ bank is divided into **five Missions**, each representing a stage in the learning journey:

1. The Launchpad – Brain Science & Crisis Awareness
2. Navigation Systems – Study Methods
3. Galactic Engineers – Thinking & Habits
4. Avoid the Black Holes – Systems & Tactics
5. Life Support & Hero State – Wellbeing & Mastery

These Missions are **progressive**, but not game levels.

---

## 3. Access Rules (Important)

A Mission may only be attempted if **learning activity has already occurred**, for example:

* a completed Pomodoro session
* a blurting session
* flashcard review
* notes created or revised

No activity → no MCQs.

This ensures MCQs measure **recall**, not guessing.

---

## 4. How a Mission Attempt Works

### Step 1: Orientation

The student is told:

* what the Mission tests
* why it matters
* that this is a *diagnostic*, not a score challenge

### Step 2: Recall Attempt

* Questions are answered **from memory**
* No notes visible
* No instant retries
* No timers or streak pressure

### Step 3: Gap Detection

Incorrect answers do **not** cause penalties.

Instead, they:

* flag a *concept name*
* create a “Knowledge Gap” marker
* generate a practice prompt (e.g. blurting or explanation)

### Step 4: Reflection

Each Mission ends with:

* one short reflective prompt (e.g. “What changed in how you think?”)
* optional retry after practice

---

## 5. MCQs Are Gates, Not Rewards

MCQs:

* do not generate XP
* do not award points
* do not unlock cosmetic rewards

Their only function is to:

* reveal understanding
* confirm readiness
* guide next practice

---

## 6. Relationship to Rank Progression

Synapse uses **three permanent ranks**:

1. Space Cadet
2. Second Officer
3. Flight Commander

MCQs interact with rank progression as follows:

* **Space Cadet**
  MCQs are used for exposure and awareness. Accuracy is not critical.

* **Second Officer**
  MCQs must show improvement over time. Reattempts should reveal fewer gaps.

* **Flight Commander**
  MCQs are optional. Priority shifts to explanation, planning, and teaching output.

As rank increases, MCQs **fade in importance**.

---

## 7. Language & Tone

Avoid framing such as:

* “Quiz”
* “Trivia”
* “Score”
* “XP”

Preferred language:

* Navigation Check
* Systems Diagnostic
* Stability Check
* Knowledge Gap Detected
* Stable Orbit

This keeps the app serious and respectful.

---

## 8. What Not to Add

Please do not add:

* leaderboards
* time pressure
* streak multipliers
* random rewards
* XP grinding
* retry-until-perfect loops

These undermine the learning intent.

---

## 9. Technical Notes

The MCQ bank is already provided in structured JSON format and can be:

* loaded as a static dataset
* filtered by Mission
* evaluated for correctness
* mapped to concept tags for gap detection

No additional content generation is required at this stage.

---

## 10. Summary (One Sentence)

The MCQ system exists to **confirm learning, not replace it**.

---

This direction preserves the quality of the existing work while ensuring Synapse remains:

* disciplined
* evidence-based
* behaviour-focused
* meaningfully different from generic study apps

Thank you for building within these constraints.
