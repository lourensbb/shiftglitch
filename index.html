What The Heck Is Going On?

ShiftGlitch is a study app built for students. It's got a cyberpunk theme because studying is more fun when it feels like hacking. Here's what everything means, in plain English:

MISSION (the home screen)

This is your starting point. Type in what you want to study (e.g. "World War 2" or "Photosynthesis") and click Inject Strat — it gives you a quick 3-step study plan using AI. You also have a to-do list and exam countdown timers here.

VAULT (study tools)

This is where you actually study. Start the Focus Timer (works like a Pomodoro — 25 min on, 5 min break). You'll also find explanations of the best study techniques, and a Q&A section with real answers about studying.

DATA SHARDS (flashcards)

Make flashcard decks to memorise stuff. Click New Deck, give it a name, then add cards (front = question, back = answer). Hit Study to quiz yourself. The app tracks which cards you know and which ones need more work.

TOOLS (the extra stuff)

Six extra study tools in this dropdown:

  • BrainDump.exe — Write everything you know about a topic from memory (without looking). Great for revision.
  • Jargon Decoder — Paste in confusing words and get a simple "explain it to an alien" breakdown.
  • Boss Fight — A timed quiz game. Answer questions correctly to beat the boss.
  • Priority Grid — Sort your tasks by what's urgent vs important (Eisenhower Matrix).
  • Anti-Lag — A tool to help you stop procrastinating and actually start studying.
  • Exploit Missions — A series of knowledge quizzes about study science. Complete them to level up.
RANK

Your progress level. You start as NPC and work your way up to System Admin by studying consistently. Complete focus sessions, flashcards, and blurt sessions to rank up. It's just for fun — but it tracks your actual effort.

BOARD, SQUAD, STATS

Board = leaderboard, see how you compare to other users. Squad = create or join a study group. Stats = charts showing how many hours you've studied, streaks, etc.

SETTINGS (the cog icon ⚙)

Set your display name (gamertag), add a Gemini AI key if you want unlimited AI features, and export/import your study data.

⚠ QUICK START — DO THIS FIRST:
  1. Go to VAULT → start a 25-min Focus Timer while you study
  2. After studying, go to TOOLS → BrainDump.exe and write what you remember
  3. Make DATA SHARDS (flashcards) for the things you keep forgetting
  4. Check MISSION to set up exam countdowns so you don't forget test dates
