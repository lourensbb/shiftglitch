# The Neural Constellation Architect

An addictive learning app that transforms studying into an **interstellar construction game**.  
Students become **Galactic Navigators** who must prevent their **knowledge stars** from fading into the void of space.

## Core Concept
Frame learning as **"Hacking the Universe of Information"** — respecting brain biology (active recall, spaced repetition, dopamine dynamics, growth mindset) while making it visually stunning and psychologically compelling.

## Main Mechanics

### 1. Retrieval Forge (Active Recall Engine)
- **Theme**: Mining for Knowledge-Energy  
- **How it works**: Upload notes → AI generates **Knowledge Asteroids** (flashcards / short quizzes)  
- **Reward**: Correct recall from memory (no peeking) creates a **Neural Spark**  
- **Rule**: Cannot mine the same asteroid twice in one day — must wait for the **Spacing Window**

### 2. Orbit Tracker (Spaced Repetition Visualizer)
- **Core mechanic**: Uses the **Forgetting Curve** as gameplay — concepts physically **drift outward** from the center over time  
- **Progression**:
  - New concepts start in **Box 1 (The Launchpad)**
  - Ignored → drifts into the **Dark Nebula** (forgetting zone)
  - Correctly recalled at increasing intervals (1 day → 3 days → 7 days → 14 days) → enters **Stable Orbit**
- **Mastery reward**: Stars in stable orbit glow permanently and add to **Galaxy Mastery Score**

### 3. Dopamine Recalibrator (Anti-Procrastination Hub)
- **Theme**: Phone = dangerous **Black Hole** threatening your galaxy  
- **Features**:
  - **15-Minute Boredom Challenge** ("Stealth Mode"): Stare at blank star-field for 15 min (no devices) to reset dopamine baseline
  - **2-Minute Entry Rocket**: Press **Ignition** → commit to just 120 seconds of work
  - **Reward**: Completing the 2 minutes grants a **Momentum Boost** (makes continuing ~80% more likely)

### 4. Babel Fish Metaphor Lab (Feynman Technique Game)
- **Mission**: "Translate this concept for a 5-year-old alien from the Andromeda Galaxy"  
- **Dual-coding bonus**: Draw a **Stupid Doodle** / simple icon for the concept  
- **Reward**: Weird + memorable pairing (e.g. angry baguette = French Revolution) earns a **Sync Badge** → doubles memory orbit stability

### 5. Yet Growth Shield (Growth Mindset Reinforcement)
- **Trigger**: Fail a quiz or mark something "too hard"  
- **Mechanic**: App forces user to rephrase status by adding **"YET"**  
  - "I don't understand this" → "I don't understand this **YET**"  
- **Impact**: Shifts identity from "failing student" to **"Galactic Explorer in Training"** (reinforces malleable intelligence)
