// modules.list

The Tools

Six study modules. Each one grounded in a different branch of cognitive science. Each one designed to make your revision actually stick.

// module_01
Leitner Flashcards

Spaced repetition done properly. Cards are sorted into five orbital boxes based on how well you know them. The system decides what to review and when — focusing your time where it actually matters.

Science: spacing effect · Leitner System · Ebbinghaus forgetting curve
// module_02
Focus Governor

A Pomodoro timer with an enforcement layer. Choose your session length, choose your intensity level, and commit to deep work. The Governor prevents shallow repetition by tracking what you actually do.

Science: Pomodoro Technique · deliberate practice · attention research
// module_03
Blurting Engine

Write down everything you know about a topic from memory. No notes. No prompts. Pure retrieval. Then compare your blurt against your notes and see exactly where your gaps are.

Science: testing effect · retrieval practice · metacognition
// module_04
Diagnostic MCQ

Knowledge gap detection. Multiple-choice questions that identify exactly what you think you know versus what you actually know. Brutal. Honest. Necessary.

Science: diagnostic assessment · confidence calibration
// module_05 · pro
Mission Architect

AI-powered study planning. Tell the system your subjects, your exam dates, and your weak areas. It generates a structured revision schedule built on the same principles as the rest of the platform.

Powered by Google Gemini · Pro feature
// module_06
Progress Intel

Your study habits tracked and visualised. Streaks, focus scores, cards mastered, blurts completed. A real record of whether you are doing the right things — not just the easy ones.

Science: habit formation · progress monitoring · motivation research
